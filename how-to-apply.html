Application Guide

Apply in 15 Minutes

Our streamlined online application is fast, secure, and 100% mobile-friendly. No printing, no faxing.

How It Works

01
Find a Listing
Browse verified rentals on our listings page. Filter by beds, price, and pet policy.
02
Complete the Application
Fill out our secure 6-step form — personal info, employment, rental history, and more.
03
Get Reviewed
We review your application within 2–3 business days. You'll receive email updates throughout.
04
Move In
Sign your lease digitally and coordinate move-in details with your landlord. Welcome home.

What You'll Need

  • Government-issued photo ID (driver's license or passport)
  • Proof of income — pay stubs, offer letter, or bank statements
  • Employment information — employer name, contact, and duration
  • Rental history — previous addresses and landlord contact info
  • Two personal or professional references
  • Social Security Number (for credit/background check)

Application Fee

A non-refundable application fee (the amount varies by property and is shown on each listing) covers your background and credit check. Our leasing team will contact you within 24 hours of submission to coordinate payment — nothing is charged at the time of application. Some properties have no application fee. Browsing and saving listings is always free.

Ready to start?

Browse listings and hit "Apply Now" on the one you love.
